Partitioning

When data is too big for one machine, split it. Partition by key-range or by hash, fight hot spots, handle secondary indexes, rebalance without downtime, and route requests to the right node.

Partitioning is about spreading data and load evenly. Key-range gives efficient range scans but risks hot spots; hashing spreads load but loses ordering. Rebalancing and routing keep it all working as you grow.
